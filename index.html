Liberate your data.

What you'll need:

  • A Divvy membership (get one here).
  • The Chrome browser (get it here).

How to use DivvyBrags:

  1. Get DivvyBrags from the Chrome store.
  2. Go to divvybikes.com, log in.
  3. Go to your Trips page. You'll see a Divvybrags sidebar with a few options.
  4. Set your data free!.


Enjoy!
